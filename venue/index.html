Venue

SBFT'23 will be held on the 14th of May, co-located with ICSE'23 in Melbourne, Australia

This year we will have a hybrid workshop. You can either attend online or in-person (Meeting Room 105).

Event Dinner

Location: Meat Market, South Wharf
Date and Time: Sunday, May 14 7:00 pm
Details: Two course meal with a 3 hour beverage package (includes wine, beer and soft drinks)
Meeting point: Meeting Room 105 at Melbourne Convention Exhibition Centre | MCEC
